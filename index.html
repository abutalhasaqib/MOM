Meme works

MOM is building an upwork for memers coined as “memeworks”. Business owners who want memes to be made for their brand, they can directly contact the members without any central control and give them gigs to create memes. Memer’s can take up this freelance work through the platform. MOM charges a very nominal fee to link memers with the buyers.

TOKEN UTILITY & ECONOMICS

MOM is token for the NFT platform. The multi-blockchain based token that serves as the basis for transactions within MOM Marketplace, and has the following uses:
